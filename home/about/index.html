about me

Hi, I'm Spencer! I design the products, systems, devices, and experiences that bring joy, create whimsy, and make the world feel a little more colorful.

My studies at Pratt Institute shaped a fast-paced, trial-and-error design process grounded in testing and understanding real user needs to make intentional decisions. Nothing makes me happier than creating intuitive, functional designs that feel meaningful to the people they're made for.

Outside my career, I have many passions in life and goals I'm working towards. Currently, I spend my free time playing and making games, listening to music, enjoying stories and trying to write my own, and learning languages of places I hope to visit. I'm also a volleyball player!

Thanks for being here, and I'll see you around the Bay Area!
